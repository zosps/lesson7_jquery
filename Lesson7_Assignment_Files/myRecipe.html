Brownies

Ingredients

  • 4 zucchini, quarted lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup freshly grate Parmesan
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees D. Coat cooling rack with nonstick spray and place on a baking sheet, set aside.

In a small bowl, combine cheese, thyme, oregano, basil, garlic poweder, salt and pepper.

Place zucchini onto prepared baking sheet.

Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with Parmesean cheese mixture.

Place into oven and bake until tender, about 15 minutes.

Broil for 2-3 minutes, or until crisp and golden brown.

Serve immediateley, garnished with parsley, if desired.
